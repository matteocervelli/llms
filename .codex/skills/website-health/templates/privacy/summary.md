# Privacy — L1 Summary

## GDPR Cookie Consent

1. **Consent banner** — Must be present, opt-IN (not pre-checked)
2. **Three actions** — Accept All / Reject All / Customize
3. **Pre-consent cookies** — ZERO non-essential cookies before consent
4. **Granular categories** — Essential, Analytics, Marketing (minimum)

## Policy Requirements

5. **Privacy policy** — Accessible from footer AND cookie banner
6. **Cookie policy** — Lists all cookies by category with purpose and duration

## Technical Requirements

7. **HTTPS enforcement** — HTTP -> HTTPS 301 redirect
8. **Set-Cookie flags** — Secure, HttpOnly, SameSite=Lax/Strict
9. **Third-party tracker inventory** — All external scripts documented

---

Ask for **patterns** for consent implementation, conditional script loading, and GDPR checklists.
