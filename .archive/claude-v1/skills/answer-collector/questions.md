# Product Assessment Questions

## WHY ARE WE BUILDING THIS? (4 questions)

### 1. Problem & Evidence
**What specific, recurring problem are we solving?** Describe 3 real instances where this problem caused measurable impact.

### 2. Strategic Priority
**If this succeeds, what measurably changes in 6 months?** What's the cost of NOT building it?

### 3. Resource Reality
**What will we STOP doing to make room for this?** What's the MVP we can ship in 4-6 weeks?

### 4. Timing
**Is this "must-build-now" or "nice-to-have-eventually"?** What's driving the urgency?

---

## WHO IS THIS FOR? (4 questions)

### 5. Primary User
**Describe the ideal user in one sentence:** their role, the moment they need this, and what they currently use today.

### 6. Access & Volume
**Can we name 10 specific potential users?** Can we access 5 of them for validation in the next 2 weeks?

### 7. Decision Economics
**Who controls the budget?** Who influences the decision? What's the typical decision timeline?

### 8. Scale Reality
**Can this serve 10 users? 100? 1,000?** What breaks at each level?

---

## WHAT MAKES IT VALUABLE? (5 questions)

### 9. Measurable Outcome
**What specific, measurable outcome does this create?**

### 10. Business Model
**How will we monetize this?** At what price point is it a "no-brainer"?

### 11. Minimum Success
**How many customers at what price do we need to justify the investment?**

### 12. Strategic Fit
**How does this complement our existing offerings?** What strategic assets does it create?

### 13. Risk Check
**If this fails, what's the real cost?** What are 3 leading indicators that would tell us to pivot or kill it?

---

## GO/NO-GO CHECKLIST (4 criteria)

### 14. Problem Validation
**We've validated the problem with 5+ potential users** (True/False)

### 15. MVP Feasibility
**We can build a useful MVP in 4-8 weeks** (True/False)

### 16. Business Model Clarity
**The business model is clear and realistic** (True/False)

### 17. Resource Commitment
**We know what we'll stop doing to make room** (True/False)
